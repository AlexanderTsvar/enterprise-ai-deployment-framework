# AI Deployment Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist before approving an AI system for production deployment.

## Business readiness

- [ ] Business objective is clearly defined
- [ ] Intended users are identified
- [ ] Success criteria are documented
- [ ] Known limitations are communicated
- [ ] Human escalation path exists

## Governance readiness

- [ ] Use case has been reviewed against internal AI policy
- [ ] Risk level has been assigned
- [ ] Responsible owner is identified
- [ ] Compliance requirements are documented
- [ ] Required approvals are complete

## Risk readiness

- [ ] AI-specific risks are documented
- [ ] Risk severity and likelihood are assessed
- [ ] Mitigations are assigned to owners
- [ ] Residual risk is accepted by accountable stakeholders
- [ ] High-risk findings are remediated or formally accepted

## Validation readiness

- [ ] Functional testing is complete
- [ ] AI evaluations are complete
- [ ] Red teaming is complete
- [ ] Guardrails have been validated
- [ ] Regression testing has been performed after mitigations

## Deployment readiness

- [ ] Deployment gate criteria are met
- [ ] Rollback plan exists
- [ ] Monitoring plan is defined
- [ ] Incident response process is documented
- [ ] Post-launch review cadence is established

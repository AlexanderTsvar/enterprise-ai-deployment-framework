# AI Observability Metrics

AI observability should go beyond infrastructure health and model performance. It should also monitor whether the AI system continues to behave safely and within acceptable risk thresholds.

## Traditional observability

- Latency
- Uptime
- Error rate
- Cost
- Throughput

## AI behavior observability

- Prompt injection success rate
- Jailbreak success rate
- Policy violation rate
- Harmful output frequency
- Sensitive information disclosure attempts
- Hallucination rate in critical workflows
- Safety regression after model updates
- User escalation rate
- Human override rate
- Time to detect and remediate critical findings

## Why it matters

AI systems evolve after deployment. Models change, prompts change, threat techniques change, and user behavior changes. Continuous monitoring helps teams detect drift, regressions, and emerging risks before they become incidents.
